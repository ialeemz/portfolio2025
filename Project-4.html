The Living Canvas

  • Homepage

  • Procedural Art Wall

An interactive digital art installation that turned guests into living artworks. Using facial detection, I transformed eyes and lips into mesmerizing animated visuals, blending technology, beauty, and creativity.

The Scope

Create an engaging experience for fashion enthusiasts that celebrates individuality while integrating tech seamlessly into a luxury event.

The Solution

We designed a multi-zone experience:

  • Art Wall: A dynamic 6x5 screen matrix displaying morphing animations of eyes and lips.
  • Makeup Station: Professional artists enhanced guests’ looks.
  • Photo Studio: Captured guests post-makeup for the transformation process.

Our custom software extracted features from each photo using OpenCV, then procedurally animated them with Python-based morphing algorithms. These were displayed in real time across 30 HD video streams, powered by Unity3D.

Behind the Scenes

  • Image Processing: OpenCV for precise eye and lip detection.
  • Generative Animations: Algorithmically morphed visuals for a unique outcome every time.
  • High-Performance Playback: Managed on advanced graphics machines for seamless performance.
  • Integrated Hardware: Components including high-resolution LED panels, cameras, and lighting for optimal visual impact.

The result? A living, breathing mosaic of human beauty and technology—continuously evolving over 5 days and drawing massive engagement.
