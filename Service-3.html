Experiential Consulting: Design, Stage, Measure

Consulting for brand activations, exhibitions, and cultural spaces. I align story, space, and systems—then deliver a plan the crew can build and operate. Outcomes are concrete: footfall, dwell, interactions, leads, and post-event insight

Processes

Discovery & Research

+
-

Clarify audience, intent, constraints, and success metrics. Map the journey (arrival → engage → convert), site realities, and operational limits. Reduce unknowns before we draw boxes.

Strategy & Concept Development

+
-

Translate goals into interaction frameworks and zones. Choose only the tech that serves the moment. Produce fast proof artifacts (flows, show-sheets, blocking, risk notes) that align creative and production.

Multi-Sensory Design

+
-

Define the touchpoints: content + control + environment. Lighting, sound, haptics, scent (where appropriate), queue logic, and reset states. Accessibility and wayfinding are built-in, not bolted on.

Execution & Delivery

+
-

Specify content pipelines, control protocols (OSC/DMX/Art-Net), trigger logic, and operator steps. Coordinate fabricators and AV. Stress-test: failure modes, recovery, and throughput at peak.

Measurement & Insights

+
-

Instrument the experience: counts, dwell, interaction depth, heatmaps, conversions/lead capture, and qualitative notes. Deliver a post-event report with what worked, what didn’t, and the next iteration.

Let’s design it clearly, stage it cleanly, and measure it.
